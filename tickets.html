Tickets

Resident college members

Current Hertford College members can purchase tickets Here.

The cost of a ticket to a current member of the college includes a full four course meal in the Sheldonain square. Resident members of the college are permitted to purchase one guest ticket. Payment for resident and guest tickets will be conducted through battels. You may select to pay the full balance of all tickets purchased, or to pay a deposit of £50 per ticket, with the full balance due in January 2014.

Alumni

Tickets are available to Old Members to reserve here. Each Old Member may reserve tickets for themselves and a guest, at the following prices:
Dining: £160
Non-Dining: £130
